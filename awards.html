Football Awards

Ballon d'Or

The most prestigious individual award in football, awarded annually to the best male player in the world.

FIFA World Cup

The most important international tournament in football, held every 4 years with national teams competing.

UEFA Champions League

The top club competition in Europe, featuring the best teams from across the continent.

UEFA Euro

Major international competition contested by European national teams every 4 years.

Copa América

The top international tournament in South America, featuring countries like Brazil and Argentina.

FIFA Club World Cup

Annual competition featuring the champion clubs from each football continent.

Copa Libertadores

The premier club competition in South America, equivalent to Europe’s Champions League.

Golden Boot

Awarded to the top goalscorer in tournaments like the World Cup, Euros, and leagues.
